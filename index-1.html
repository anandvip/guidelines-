STEP 1 • BEFORE THE SALE

Pre-Sale Planning Checklist

Complete these critical steps before proceeding with the land sale to ensure legal clarity, tax optimisation, and smooth fund deployment.

📄

1. Land Documentation Verification

  • Verify title deed, patta, registry
  • Confirm agricultural land classification (tax exemption)
  • Check encumbrances, liens, or disputes
  • Obtain updated 7/12 extract & mutation entries
📏

2. Professional Land Valuation

  • Hire certified land valuer
  • Cross-reference recent local sales
  • Check current circle rates
  • Obtain multiple valuations
⚖️

3. Legal Consultation

  • Consult property lawyer
  • Verify capital gains tax exemption
  • Review remaining land contract
  • Understand state-specific agricultural land sale rules
📊

4. Tax Planning Consultation

  • Meet agricultural income tax advisor
  • Document current tax-exempt status
  • Plan transition to taxable investment income

Action Plan for Implementing This Strategy

Immediate Pre-Sale Actions (1-2 Months Before Sale)

  1. Open separate transaction & investment bank accounts (SBI, HDFC, ICICI)
  2. Open demat account (ICICI Direct / Zerodha / HDFC Securities) & complete KYC
  3. Consult certified financial planner, tax advisor & lawyer

First Week After Fund Receipt

Deposit funds safely → Move ₹10L to liquid funds → Open SCSS & POMIS accounts.

Week 2–4 & Month 2

Implement FD ladder → Invest in debt funds & SWP → Purchase tax-free & corporate bonds.

Ongoing Management

  • Monthly Income consolidation account with auto-transfers
  • Quarterly Performance review & SWP adjustment
  • Annually Full portfolio review with CFP

Risk Assessment and Mitigation Strategies

Interest Rate Risk

Mitigated by laddering maturities and diversification across instruments.

Inflation Risk

Annual review of withdrawal rates + partial allocation to inflation-indexed options.

Longevity Risk

Conservative withdrawal rate + surplus reinvestment + retained appreciating land assets.

Tax Policy Risk

Diversified tax treatment + annual consultation with tax professional.
